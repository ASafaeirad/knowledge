A "riveting conversation" is one that is so extremely interesting and engaging that it holds your complete attention, making it difficult to look away or think of anything else. It's a discussion that is captivating and engrossing, whether it's because of a compelling story, unexpected twists, or a powerful connection between the speakers. 

The word "riveting" originates from the process of fastening materials with a "rivet," which securely holds them in place, just as a riveting conversation holds your focus.
