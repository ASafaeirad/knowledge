Adrenal refers to anything related to the adrenal glands two small glands located on top of the kidneys.

![[Adrenal Gland.png]]
The adrenal glands are part of the [[Endocrine System]], and they produce hormones that help regulate:

Stress response (e.g., [[adrenaline]] and [[cortisol]])
- Metabolism
- Blood pressure
- Immune system
- Electrolyte balance

Each gland has two main parts:
1. Adrenal cortex: produces [[Steroid Hormones]] like [[cortisol ]] and [[Aldosterone]].
2. Adrenal medulla: produces catecholamines like [[adrenaline]] (epinephrine) and [[Noradrenaline]] (norepinephrine).
